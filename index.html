Logic Manual is a relatively brief introduction to logic. I have tried to focus on the core topics and have neglected some issues that are covered in more comprehensive books such as Forbes (1994), Guttenplan (1997), Hodges (2001), Smith (2003), and Tennant (1990). In particular, I have tried not to include material that is inessential to Preliminary Examinations and Moderations in Oxford.
